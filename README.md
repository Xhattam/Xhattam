# Hi, I'm Jess 👋

## Whats a Xhattam 🤔

There's a little story there. 

- Context: There's a French writer I liked a lot named [Maxime Chattam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maxime_Chattam).
- Problem: I was signing up to a website (maybe even this one) and couln't find an available username. 
- Resolution: I tried this writer's name in a last ditch attempt and... made a typo.

BOOM. ✨ Xhattam ✨. Available, all the time, everywhere. I aknowledge it's easier to read than to pronounce (/ksatam/).

But don't hurt yourself, Jess is fine.

## Projects

### SideCook

A personal interest project, born out of trying to save food and money using [Olio](https://olioapp.com/en/) and finding myself with odd items I wouldn't have bought organically.
I stared inside my fridge at that weird cabbage or that raw beetroot, wanting to use them, not always knowing what to make with them.

I was falling behind with AI tools, thought I could combine things a bit.

Currently, SideCook is in early stages of development, but once it's at a stage I'm proud of, I'll deploy it somewhere.

### Royal Berkshire Archives Automation App (RBA3)
